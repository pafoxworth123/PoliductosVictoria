## 📋 About the Company

**Poliductos Victoria** is a leading Mexican manufacturer and supplier of agricultural plastic products. We specialize in:

- 🌊 **Poliducto** (agricultural polyd pipe) - all diameters, C-40 and C-80
- 💧 **Manguera de riego** - 16mm irrigation hose
- 🌱 **Bolsas para vivero** - nursery bags
- 🗑️ **Bolsas de basura** - garbage bags (60×90, 90×120)
- ♻️ **Plásticos reciclados** - recycled plastic products
- 🚜 **Bolsa conductora de agua** - water conduction bags
- 👕 **Bolsa camiseta** - t-shirt bags
- 📦 **Peletizado** - plastic pelletizing

**We serve:** Puebla, Veracruz, Oaxaca, Tlaxcala, and 10+ more states.
